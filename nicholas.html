Nicholas

A Story of Resilience

Nicholas is a heartfelt zine that tells the story of my grandfather, a refugee during World War II. Through captivating narratives and visuals, it chronicles his life before, during, and after the war, offering a profound insight into his experiences and the resilience he showed. Nicholas serves as an inspiring reminder for readers to cherish and engage with the stories and lives of their own grandparents, fostering a deeper appreciation for family history and the lessons it holds.
