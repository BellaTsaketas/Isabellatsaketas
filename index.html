About Me

Isabella, currently based in Melbourne, Australia, is a final-year university student pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Design (Digital Media). Her academic journey has sparked a keen interest in the intersection of healthcare and design, motivating her to explore innovative solutions that improve well-being. Isabella is also passionate about fostering greater inclusivity in motorsports, particularly in Formula 1, as she believes in the importance of gender equality and empowerment in the field. Isabella specializes in user experience (UX) design, striving to create intuitive and impactful digital experiences. She also focuses on user interface (UI) design, aiming to enhance the aesthetic appeal and user interaction of her projects. Alongside her design skills, Isabella has a talent for sound design and composition, integrating her musical background into her design work to create immersive experiences. Isabella's professional approach is characterized by her collaborative nature and leadership skills, honed through her experiences in team environments. Recognized for her ability to deliver high-quality results under pressure, Isabella consistently meets deadlines while pushing the boundaries of creativity and innovation.
