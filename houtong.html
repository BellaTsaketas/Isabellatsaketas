Bubble Tea Cafe Website Design

I designed a captivating website for a bubble tea cafe nestled in the heart of Leeds, aiming to mirror its cosy atmosphere yet capture the fun and playful spirit of bubble tea, as requested by the owners. I curated custom iconography for the menu, designing all of the drinks and toppings. Using a mobile-first design approach, I ensured the website was intuitively structured for seamless navigation across mobile, tablet, and desktop devices. This strategy prioritised user experience, making it easy for visitors to explore the cafe's offerings and engage with its content effortlessly. Additionally, I incorporated a dedicated page for booking the event space upstairs in the shop, meeting the owner's requirement for a streamlined booking system. This approach not only highlighted the cafe's unique charm but also enhanced its online presence, providing a delightful digital experience for customers.
