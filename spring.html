The Spring Quest

Rescore, Foley and Sound Design

For the short film The Spring Quest, I rescoured the animation and created new sound design, aiming to create an ethereal and immersive audio experience using instruments such as the flute and Celtic harp. Inspired by John Powell's composition work, I assigned specific instruments to characters, maintaining a consistent thematic representation throughout the piece. This approach included call-and-response rhythms to highlight character interactions, crafting an engaging and dynamic auditory experience that harmoniously supported the film's visuals. To enhance the grandeur of the score, I modulated the piece from C major Pentatonic to A Major, adding depth and richness to the overall sound.
