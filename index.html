About Me

Isabella, based in Melbourne, Australia, is a final year university student pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Design (Digital Media), with a keen interest in the intersection of healthcare and design. Passionate about motorsports, she is dedicated to increasing women's participation in the field. Specializing in user experience and interaction design, as well as sound design and composition, Isabella is also a musician and composer. Her work showcases a blend of creativity and technical skill, honed through her experience in collaborative team environments and leadership roles. Known for her ability to meet deadlines while delivering high-quality results, she is committed to pushing boundaries and creating impactful design solutions.
